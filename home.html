    WELCOME TO ABC SCHOOL

    A warm welcome to Canossa High School. Canossa School situated in Mahim, was started in 1932 as a Primary School, annexed to the Parish of Our Lady of Victories. It was recognized as a High School in 1946.

    Canossa High School is a Catholic School for Girls, established and administered by the Canossian Daughters of Charity. The School is under the jurisdiction of the Archbishop of Mumbai.

    The course of instruction includes all the subjects prescribed by the Government for English Teaching Schools, in Maharashtra State. The school is subject to inspection by the Department of Education, Government of Maharashtra. The School comprises of ten standards and prepares pupils for the S.S.C. Examination.

    Intellectual training is imparted through instruction aiming at academic excellence, helping them to think for themselves and arrive at the right judgment. Physical Education is promoted with the purpose of developing a sound mind in a healthy body. Programmes offered are Physical Training, Apparatus Drill and special coaching in athletics, basket-ball, throw-ball, karate etc. Emotional and social training is encouraged through the House System, Class Democracy, Leadership, Teamwork, and Works of Mercy conducted through the St. Magdalene of Canossa Club and Social Service Classes. Cultural training is fostered through co-curricular activities like Dramatics, Dance, Elocution, Singing, etc. We aim at training our pupils to be simple, honest, kind and cheerful, both in school and at home, ever-ready to be of service to the needy and the down-trodden, to be proud citizens of their country and to be inspiring messengers of change in the world of today.
